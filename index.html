OUR STORY

We are Brooklyn Candle Studio. We create otherworldly botanical fragrances inspired by the beauty of nature and the wonder of travel. Our in-house artisans make our candles, diffusers, and room mists in our Brooklyn workshop using soy wax and phthalate-free perfume oils infused with essential oils. We pride ourselves on our use of sustainable materials, ethical production, and meticulous craftsmanship.
